Time and habits

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

During yesterday's and today's sessions I tried putting myself in a very positive and bright environment as well as removing most distractions like email or Facebook. I also tried having a break every 50-60 minutes.

What did you find interesting?

I found it amazing how my brain just stayed with the topic I was reading and learning about for a good few hours.

What worked for you?

It was simply great having no disruptions for a while.

Which will you experiment further with?

I think I might give meditations a try to achieve better focus rather than getting distracted all the time.
